About Jewellery Shop

We work in design studios, creating new jewelry designs for manufacturers or clients.We must have a strong understanding of the properties of precious metals and the ability to use a variety of tools and techniques to create beautiful and durable jewelry.

Some of the key tasks that we perform in our work include:-
-Melting and casting gold and other precious metals
-Forging and shaping metal using hammers and other tools
-Soldering and joining pieces of metal together
-Setting gemstones and other decorative elements
-Polishing and finishing jewelry
-Repairing and restoring damaged jewelry
